<p>美國總統川普6月18日在「真實社群」發文稱，蘋果已同意與英特爾合作，在美國設計和製造其晶片。（路透）</p>
<p>U.S. President Donald Trump said in a Truth Social post on Thursday that Apple has agreed to work with Intel to design and manufacture its chips in the United States.</p>
<p>美國總統川普週四在「真實社群」發文稱，蘋果已同意與英特爾合作，在美國設計和製造其晶片。</p>

<p>A partnership with Intel helps Apple diversify its manufacturing base as it seeks additional chip capacity. Apple relies heavily on TSMC, whose advanced production lines are in high demand from AI chipmakers such as Nvidia and AMD.</p>
<p>與英特爾的合作，有助於蘋果實現製造基地多元化，以尋求額外的晶片產能。蘋果高度依賴台積電，輝達和超微半導體等人工智慧晶片製造商，非常需要台積電的先進生產線。</p>
<p>Intel reached a preliminary deal to make some chips for Apple after more than a year of discussions, the Wall Street Journal reported in May.</p>
<p>華爾街日報5月報導，在經過逾1年磋商後，英特爾與蘋果達成初步協議，將為其製造部分晶片。</p>
<p>An Apple contract gives Intel a steady demand, boosting both its reputation and a manufacturing business that has lagged TSMC in recent years.</p>
<p>取得蘋果的合約，將為英特爾帶來穩定需求，進而提升其聲譽，並改善近年來落後於台積電的製造業務。</p>
