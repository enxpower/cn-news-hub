<p>山姆．尼爾逝世引發全球影迷悼念。（路透）</p>
<p>Sam Neill, the New Zealand actor best known for playing paleontologist Dr Alan Grant in dinosaur blockbuster "Jurassic Park" and whose career included more than 50 movies, has died at the age of 78.</p>
<p>以恐龍賣座巨片「侏儸紀公園」古生物學家艾倫．葛蘭博士一角廣為人知，生涯演出包括超過50部電影的紐西蘭演員山姆．尼爾逝世，享壽78歲。</p>

<p>A post shared on social media by his family said Neill’s death ​in Sydney "was sudden and unexpected but blessed by the fact that Sam remained cancer free." In April, Neill announced he was ‌cancer free after a public battle with blood cancer.</p>
<p>他的家人在社群媒體發布貼文，說明尼爾在雪梨過世「突然且出乎意料，但也因山姆未再受到癌症困擾感到慶幸」。尼爾4月宣布，在對外公開與血癌搏鬥後擺脫了癌症的威脅。</p>
<p>Described by critics as "versatile" and "reliably excellent", Neill landed starring roles across many genres, ranging from a submarine officer in the 1990 action-thriller "The Hunt for Red October" to the anti-Christ in 1981’s Omen III.</p>
<p>尼爾被影評家描述為「多才多藝」及「表現穩健」，他主演的角色橫跨諸多類型，從1990年動作驚悚片「獵殺紅色十月」的潛艦軍官，到1981年的反基督電影「天魔3」。</p>
<p>He also played countless anguished husbands, including opposite Holly Hunter in the Oscar-winning "The Piano" (1993) .</p>
<p>他還飾演過無數飽受煎熬的丈夫角色，包括1993年奧斯卡獲獎電影「鋼琴師和她的情人」中與荷莉．杭特對戲。</p>
<p>versatile：形容詞，多方位的、多功能的。例句：He has versatile knowledge ranging from history to science.（他通曉從歷史到科學等領域的多方面知識。）</p>
<p>anguished：形容詞，痛苦的、悲痛的。例句：The anguished parents who lost their sons on the battlefield protested against this war.（在戰場上失去兒子的悲痛父母們示威反對這場戰爭。）</p>
