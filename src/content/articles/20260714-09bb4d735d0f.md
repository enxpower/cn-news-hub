<p>警方在雪梨機場執行勤務。（歐新社）</p>
<p>◎ 管淑平</p>
<p>A bomb disposal unit was called to an Australian airport on Thursday － only to find the suspicious object was a laser hair removal device.</p>
<p>澳洲1座機場週四召來炸彈拆除小組，結果發現，可疑物品只是1支雷射除毛器。</p>
<p>請繼續往下閱讀...</p>
<p>Avalon airport in Victoria state was partially evacuated in the early hours after the item was detected during a security screening.</p>
<p>位於維多利亞州的愛華隆機場當天凌晨局部疏散，在安檢偵測到這件物品後。</p>
<p>"The Bomb Response Unit conducted checks on an item, and it was determined to be a laser hair removal device," Victoria police told AFP in a statement.</p>
<p>「爆裂物處理小組對1件物品進行檢查，確認那是1支雷射除毛器」，維多利亞州警方在聲明中告訴法新社。</p>
<p>Acting Inspector Nick Uebergang said that the suspicious device － the laser hair removal tool － along with a hot chocolate container, was kept on a conveyor belt while police were called.</p>
<p>代理警督尼克‧烏伯甘說，通報警方時，這件可疑裝置、也就是雷射除毛工具，和1個熱巧克力容器正一起被放在輸送帶上。</p>
<p>"The person who had the bag wasn’t too cooperative with us to start off with too, which made things a little bit difficult," he said.</p>
<p>「持有那個包包的人一開始不太配合我們，一度讓事情有點棘手」，他說。</p>
<p>The man was not charged and the airport reopened later in the day. (AFP)</p>
<p>該名男子未被起訴，機場也在當天稍晚重新開放。（法新社）</p>
