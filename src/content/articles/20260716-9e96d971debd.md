<p>美國總統川普以鑄造成本過高為由，宣布停止鑄造1美分硬幣。（路透檔案照）</p>
<p>◎ 張沛元</p>
<p>Months after the last of the United States’ one-cent coins were pressed, some states are beginning to offer their own two cents on the penny problem by setting rounding guidance for cash purchases.</p>
<p>在美國鑄造出最後一批1美分硬幣的幾個月後，一些州開始就解決1美分硬幣短缺問題各自發揮創意，針對現金交易制定四捨五入指南。</p>

<p>Some retailers began pulling out all the stops just to scrounge up some pennies.</p>
<p>部分零售商開始拚命蒐集1美分硬幣。</p>
<p>Major retailers took varied approaches.</p>
<p>大型零售業者則採取各種不同措施。</p>
<p>One solution to the penny problem is rounding to the nearest nickel. If the final price, after taxes, ends in one, two, six or seven cents, payments in cash round down. For example, $1.91 or $1.92 becomes $1.90. If the price ends in three, four, eight or nine, cash payments round up. For $1.98 or $1.99, the consumer pays $2.</p>
<p>其中一種辦法是（將消費總額）四捨五入到最接近的5美分。若稅後最終價格的尾數為1、2、6或7美分，現金支付的金額便向下取整。例如，1.91美元或1.92美元變成1.90美元。若價格尾數為3、4、8或9美分，現金支付則向上取整。例如，1.98美元或1.99美元時，消費者要付2美元。</p>
<p>(someone’s) two cents：慣用語，某人的看法或意見，或指個人淺見。例句：If you want my two cents, I think you should quit your job as soon as possible.（你如果想聽我的淺見，那我覺得你應該盡快離職。）</p>
<p>scrounge up：片語，四處摸索搜尋才找到……；scrounge，動詞，白要，尋找。例句：The old man managed to scrounge up enough money for a cup of coffee.（老人好不容易湊夠錢買1杯咖啡。）</p>
