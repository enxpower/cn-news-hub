<p>據傳中國計畫放行尖端AI公司採購輝達H200晶片。（路透）</p>
<p>◎ 魏國金</p>
<p>China is planning to allow the country’s top AI companies to buy a limited number of Nvidia’s H200 chips, ​the Information reported, citing two people with direct knowledge ‌of the matter.</p>
<p>《The Information》引述2名知情者報導，中國計畫允許該國的頂尖AI公司採購少量輝達H200晶片。</p>

<p>Chinese officials have told Alibaba, ByteDance and DeepSeek in recent weeks that they may soon receive permission to buy some H200 chips, the report said.</p>
<p>報導說，近幾週來，中國官員已告知阿里巴巴、字節跳動與深度求索，或許很快就會取得採購一些H200晶片的許可。</p>
<p>The U.S. government has allowed Nvidia to sell its advanced H200 chips to China, and licensed about 10 Chinese firms to buy the ​chips. However, Chinese officials, keen to nurture domestic suppliers, have withheld approval so ​far.</p>
<p>美國政府已允許輝達向中國銷售其先進的H200晶片，並核發約10家中企採購該晶片的許可。然而，熱衷於扶植本土供應商的中國官員，迄今仍未予批准。</p>
<p>Beijing is still determining the exact number of Nvidia chips to approve, and it could amount to fewer than 200,000 ​in total, the ​Information said, adding that was less than half of what the companies requested earlier this year.</p>
<p>《The Information》指出，北京仍在決定輝達晶片的確切批准數量，總數可能少於20萬顆，並補充，此數量不到這些公司今年初申請的一半。</p>
