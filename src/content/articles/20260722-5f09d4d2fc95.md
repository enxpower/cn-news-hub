<p>中國北京1家購物中心搭建假教室，舉行「上課偷吃」比賽，讓成年人重溫他們叛逆的童年。（示意圖，法新社檔案照）</p>
<p>Sitting in a fake classroom mocked-up in a Beijing mall, hundreds of Chinese adults spent the weekend reliving their rebellious childhoods as they took part in a "school sneak-eating" contest.</p>
<p>數百名中國成年人週末齊聚北京1家購物中心搭建的假教室，透過參加1場「上課偷吃」比賽，重溫他們叛逆的童年。</p>
<p>請繼續往下閱讀...</p>
<p>Using everything from textbooks and water bottles to pieces of paper as cover, participants competed to see if they could scoff a variety of snacks right under the teacher-referee’s nose.</p>
<p>參賽者利用從教科書、水瓶到紙張等各種物品做為掩護，競相看誰能在充當裁判的老師眼皮底下，狼吞虎嚥地吃下各種零食。</p>
<p>Sunday’s competition, organised by the mall and publicised online, is just one iteration of a nostalgia-driven craze that has taken Chinese social media by storm in recent weeks.</p>
<p>這場由該購物中心主辦並在網路上宣傳的週日競賽，只是近幾週席捲中國社群媒體的一股懷舊狂熱的其中一種表現形式。</p>
<p>Those who managed to finish all their snacks within 10 minutes without being caught were declared victors and progressed to the next round. (AFP)</p>
<p>那些在10分鐘內吃完所有零食、而且沒被抓到的參賽者，被宣布為獲勝者，並晉級下一輪比賽。（法新社）</p>
<p>rebellious︰形容詞，叛逆的。例句：He went through a rebellious phase as a teenager.（他在青少年時期經歷一段叛逆期。）</p>
<p>scoff︰動詞，狼吞虎嚥地吃。例句：He scoffed his dinner and rushed out.（他狼吞虎嚥地吃完晚餐就衝了出去。）</p>
