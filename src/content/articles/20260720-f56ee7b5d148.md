![](https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/26-07-20/449bc7f1-c826-4d1e-b05d-ec3b49bcd910.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85)

<p label="图片备注">结果我一查，一袋软糖里</p>
<p label="图片备注">GABA含量满打满算也就63mg</p>
<p label="图片备注">有机构测过，单次摄入75mgGABA</p>
<p label="图片备注">才能稍微舒缓情绪，改善焦虑</p>
<p label="图片备注">100-300mg才能到助眠这一步</p>

![](https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/26-07-20/f18c8bd8-4850-4ec8-8778-ff4624619a27.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85)
