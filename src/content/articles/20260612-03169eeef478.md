<p>José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero, already under investigation for alleged influence-peddling, facing questions over items found in office safe</p><p>The former Spanish prime minister José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero is being investigated for possible tax fraud and smuggling after police discovered jewellery valued at more than €1.3m (£1.1m) while searching his office safe as part of a separate inquiry.</p><p>Zapatero, who led two socialist governments between 2004 and 2011, is already under investigation <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/may/19/spanish-ex-pm-criminal-investigation-bailout-airline-rodriguez-zapatero">for alleged influence-peddling and other offences</a> relating to the state bailout of the Spanish Plus Ultra airline during the Covid pandemic.
