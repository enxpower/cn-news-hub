<p>輝達執行長黃仁勳表示，利用走私零件拼湊而成的黑市資料中心，是「死路一條」。（歐新社）</p>
<p>◎盧永山</p>
<p>Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang told shareholders on Wednesday that if a commercial opportunity conflicts with U.S. national security, the company would prioritize American interests.</p>
<p>輝達執行長黃仁勳週三對股東表示，如果商業機會與美國國家安全發生衝突，該公司將優先考量美國的利益。</p>

<p>He added that if a company wanted to smuggle Nvidia’s chips or systems into countries with export restrictions — such as China — they would have challenges getting it working because Nvidia wouldn’t provide support or repairs.</p>
<p>黃仁勳還說，若有公司想走私輝達的晶片或系統，到美國實施出口管制的國家，例如中國，他們想要讓這些晶片或系統運作將面臨困難，因為輝達不會提供支援或維修。</p>
<p>“Advanced AI data centers are massive integrated systems that require trusted hardware, software, networking, and continuing support,” Huang said. “Trying to cobble together data centers with some smuggled products is a dead end.”</p>
<p>黃仁勳表示：「先進AI資料中心是巨大整合的系統，需要可靠的軟硬體、聯網和持續的支援，試圖把資料中心和一些走私產品拼湊在一起，將是死路一條。」</p>
<p>Washington regulators and the Trump administration are increasingly wary that exporting AI software and hardware to China and other nations is a threat to national security.</p>
<p>華盛頓監管機構和川普政府越來越擔心，向中國和其他敵對國家出口人工智慧（AI）軟硬體，將危及美國的國家安全。</p>
<p>prioritize：動詞，優先考慮、確定優先順序。例句：You must learn to prioritize your work.（你必須學會優先處理最重要的工作 。）</p>
<p>cobble：名詞，鵝卵石；動詞，拼湊、修理（鞋子）。例句：Tom learned to cobble from his grandfather.（湯姆從祖父那裡學會修鞋。）</p>
